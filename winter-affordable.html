In The City

The Grotto Lights

  • Walk through the Festival of Lights at the Grotto for some festive holiday fun.
  • Best time to go: Year-round, but the Chinese New Year celebration is January-February.

Chinese New Year at Lan Su Chinese Garden

  • The two-week festival features hanging red lanterns, performances, light displays, and activities.
  • Best time to go: December.

Ice Skating at Lloyd Center

  • Rent some skates and enjoy this conveniently located rink!
  • Best time to go: Year-round, but extra nice around the holidays.

Portland Art Museum

  • Stroll through exhibits at the Portland Art Museum.
  • Best time to go: Year-round.
  • Tip: There are free entry days quarterly!

DIY Bar

  • Craft workshop meets bar in this beloved Portland date night. Sip and craft, paint your pet, wood burn coasters, make charcuterie board, and more!
  • Best time to go: Year-round.
  • Tip: Check their calendar for what appeals to you, and book well in advance!

OMSI After Dark

  • Enjoy the Oregon Museum of Science and Industry after regular hours in the 21+ event. There are often DJs and drag shows, so make an evening out of it!
  • Best time to go: Year-round.
  • Tip: They often give out free drinks and snacks, so don’t go too full! Check the calendar for events that might appeal to you.

Studio Ghibli Film Festival

  • Over thirty days, audiences will have the opportunity to experience eighteen extraordinary adventures from one of the most acclaimed animation studios in the world.
  • Best time to go: March.

Helium Comedy Club

  • This comedy club in Portland is where the biggest names come to perform! Laugh your ass off and come hungry or thirsty as there is a two-purchase minimum per person.
  • Best time to go: Year-round.
  • Tip: Get there early for good seats.

Soak in the Kennedy School Soaking Pool

  • A great place to spend an hour soaking and talking in this amazing pool!
  • Best time to go: Spring, fall, and winter.
  • Tip: Pair with a movie or a drink in detention also at the Kennedy School!

Mox Boarding House

  • Eat dinner and play games - what sounds better than that?
  • Best time to go: Year-round.
  • Tip: Ask the experts for a game recommendation and learn a new game when you go!

Glowing Greens PDX

  • Experience Portland’s pirate-themed blacklight indoor mini golf bar.
  • Best time to go: Year-round.
  • Tip: Make reservations!

Oaks Park Roller Rink

  • Not only is Oaks the oldest roller rink in the country, it also has the largest skating floor west of the Mississippi!
  • Best time to go: Year-round, but perfect for a rainy day!
  • Tip: Tuesday nights are adult-only and feature a live DJ!

Ground Kontrol

  • Have a date night at this beloved Arcade Bar.
  • Best time to go: Year-round, but perfect for a rainy day!
  • Tip: Check the calendar before going as they are occasionally closed for private events.

Rimsky-Korsakoffee House

  • Open 7pm-12am, this late-night dessert and coffee house is supposedly haunted. Before a night out, enjoy this mildly threatening weird spot, loved by locals. Named for the Russian composer Nikolai Rimsky-Korsakov, the entire place is classical music-themed with tables named for deceased composers.
  • Best time to go: Year-round.
  • Tip: Be sure to bring cash as credit cards aren’t accepted here. Also, tip the entertainment and don’t expect overly friendly service from the waitstaff. And don’t leave before checking out the bathroom!

Sofar Sounds Show

  • See an intimate live music show in a secret location.
  • Tip: Check upcoming shows to find one in your neighborhood!

Further Out

Cut down your own Christmas tree in Mount Hood National Forest

  • Create memories and explore the forest while hunting for a Christmas tree this holiday season! Whether you prefer a Noble fir, a Pacific silver, or Douglas firs, you're sure to find a beautiful tree in Mt. Hood National Forest.
  • Drive time from Portland: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Best time to go: December.
  • Tip: Your permit allows cutting one Christmas tree up to 15 feet tall! Check online to find areas that are prohibited from cutting.

Bagby Hot Springs

  • These hot springs in Mount Hood National Forest are set to reopen in 2023!
  • Drive time from Portland: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Best time to go: Year round, but winter sees fewer crowds as the campground nearby closes after Labor Day.
  • Tip: Go early in the morning during the week to avoid most crowds. If you go in winter, make sure to check road conditions!
