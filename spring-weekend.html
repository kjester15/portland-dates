Hood River, Oregon

Yacht Tubs

  • Best time to go: Year-round on a clear day! The shoulder seasons would be particularly great.
  • Tip: Split the cost with a group of friends!

Detroit, Oregon

Envi Adventures

  • Best time to go: Year-round on a clear day! In Spring it could be especially nice because there’s no smoke and the mountains are still inaccessible, so view their snow-capped peaks from above!
  • Tip: They have lots of promotions so sign up for their emails for a deal!
