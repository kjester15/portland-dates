Spring is a beautiful time in the PNW but some it feels like it’s dragging. The nice days are really nice, but when it rains, it pours. Spend clear days frolicking in fields of wildflowers and force yourself outside on crummy ones by hiking to waterfalls when their flow is highest – I promise it’ll be worth it! Do more indoor winter activities on weeks that have no sun in sight, and when the good weather actually last, take a weekend trip to help you feel sane, and mentally prepare yourself for the busyness that is summer.
