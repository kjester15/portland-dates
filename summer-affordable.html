In The City

Portland Vegan Beer & Food Festival

  • This event features the region's best craft beer, vegan eats, live music, and more.
  • Best time to go: This event takes place in June.

Further Out

Dahlia Festival at Swan Island Dahlia's

  • Stroll through almost 50 acres and a beautiful display garden of stunning dahlias featuring over 370 varieties.
  • Best time to go: August-September.

Hood River Lavender Farm

  • Every July this farm hosts the annual Lavender Daze Festival. This event brings thousands to celebrate the peak lavender bloom of the season. Live music, food, art, and activities vendors also join in on the fun. 2022 will be the 18th annual Lavender Daze Festival.
  • Best time to go: Late June - Early July.
  • Tip: Pair with a visit to a winery, like Stave & Stone Winery which shares the space. This winery has an outdoor bar that overlooks its lavender fields, Mt. Hood, and Mount Adams
