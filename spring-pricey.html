In The City

Yacht Tubs

  • Best time to go: Year-round on a clear day! The shoulder seasons would be particularly great.
  • Tip: Split the cost with a group of friends!

Further Out

Envi Adventures

  • Best time to go: Year-round on a clear day! In Spring it could be especially nice because there’s no smoke and the mountains are still inaccessible, so view their snow-capped peaks from above!
  • Tip: They have lots of promotions so sign up for their emails for a deal!

Take a Mountain Biking Lesson

  • Find out if you like mountain biking by taking a lesson offered through REI where they’ll fit you with a mountain bike. There are so many great trails within one hour and beyond from Portland, and it’s an amazing activity to enjoy year-round.
  • Tip: Spring, so if you like it you can buy your own bike and go throughout summer and fall!
